PANDA

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) is a bear species classified in the genus Ailuropoda, native to south central China. It is characterized by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The giant panda is a conservation reliant vulnerable species, with only about 1,864 individuals living in the wild as of 2014. The panda's diet consists almost exclusively of bamboo, which makes up 99% of its diet. They are also known for their distinctive vocalizations, which include bleats, honks, and barks.
